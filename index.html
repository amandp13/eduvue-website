    What is the problem we are solving?

    There are lot of free CRMs in market however they don’t address Education Industry specific problems listed below.

    Problems for:


    Educational Institutes:


  • Apart from big players, small players work on very thin margins to operate their institutes.
  • Hence when it comes to technology, even after knowing its benefits, they lack investment capability and technology know-how to implement the same and reap benefits.
  • Multiple tools for managing business. Increases cost of managing different tools and reduces efficiencies. Integration between different tools are also not easy.
  • Not able to analyse student’s performance and thereby a teacher’s efficiency.
  • Increase reach out to new students.


    • Teachers of these Institutes:


  • Most of the teachers using blackboard to explain concepts. In such cases sharing, storing and managing their classroom notes becomes tedious.
  • They spend lot of time evaluating physical answer sheet of students instead of focusing on student learning.
  • During a class session, they are unable to quickly identify if students have understood the concept or not.
  • Not able to track student performance with detailed insights.


    • Students of these Institutes:


  • They spend maximum time of classroom in copying what teacher has written on the board rather than spending time in understanding the concept.
  • In case they miss a class or 2, have to depend on other students for notes and concept clearance.
